Getting Started with Your Garden

Starting a garden is easier than you think! You don't need a big yard or expensive tools to grow your own vegetables.

What You'll Need

  • A sunny spot (6+ hours of sunlight daily)
  • Good soil or potting mix
  • Seeds or small plants
  • Water source nearby
  • Basic tools: shovel, watering can

First Steps

  1. Choose your garden spot
  2. Prepare the soil
  3. Plant your seeds
  4. Water regularly
  5. Watch them grow!

Best Plants for Beginners

These vegetables are forgiving and grow quickly, perfect for your first garden experience.

Easy Vegetables to Grow
Plant Time to Harvest Special Tips
Lettuce 30-45 days Grows in cool weather
Radishes 25-30 days Very fast growing
Tomatoes 70-80 days Need support stakes

Pro tip: Start with just 2-3 types of plants. It's better to grow a few plants well than many plants poorly!
